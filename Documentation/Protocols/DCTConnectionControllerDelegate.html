DCTConnectionControllerDelegate Protocol Reference

Conforms to NSObject
Declared in DCTConnectionController.h

Overview

Protocol for delegates of DCTConnectionController to conform to.

The delegate of DCTConnectionController must adopt the DCTConnectionControllerDelegate protocol, although all the methods are optional. They allow the delegate to handle only certain types of events, although connectionController:didSucceedWithObject: and connectionController:didFailWithError: should both be handled to take advantage of the data and handle any occuring errors.

Tasks

  • - connectionController:didSucceedWithObject: Tells the delegate the connection has succeeded.
  • - connectionController:didFailWithError: Tells the delegate the connection has failed.
  • - connectionControllerWasCancelled: Tells the delegate the connection was cancelled.
  • - connectionController:didReceiveResponse: Tells the delegate a response has been received from the server.
